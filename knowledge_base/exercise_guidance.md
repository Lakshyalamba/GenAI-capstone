# Exercise Recommendations
Keywords: exercise, activity, walking, fitness, heart rate, endurance, rehab

Regular movement improves blood pressure, weight control, cholesterol patterns, insulin sensitivity, and overall cardiovascular conditioning.

## Weekly targets
- Build toward at least 150 minutes of moderate activity each week if there is no clinician-imposed restriction.
- Start with walking, cycling, or low-impact cardio if conditioning is low and progress gradually.
- Add two strength sessions each week to support metabolic health and body composition.

## Safety notes
- Stop and seek assessment if exercise repeatedly causes chest pain, severe dizziness, or unusual shortness of breath.
- Patients with exercise-induced symptoms may need clinician guidance before increasing intensity.
