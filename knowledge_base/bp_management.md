# Blood Pressure Management
Keywords: blood pressure, bp, hypertension, sodium, monitoring, medication, home cuff

Elevated blood pressure increases cardiovascular strain and should be tracked consistently rather than occasionally.

## Key actions
- Use a validated home blood pressure cuff and record readings at similar times of day.
- Reduce excess sodium, highly processed foods, and large swings in weight or alcohol intake.
- If readings remain high over time, discuss medication review and risk-factor management with a clinician.

## Follow-up triggers
- Persistent systolic readings above the clinician's target deserve formal follow-up.
- Sudden severe blood pressure elevation with chest pain, neurologic symptoms, or severe shortness of breath needs urgent care.
