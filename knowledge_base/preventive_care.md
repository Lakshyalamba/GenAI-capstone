# Preventive Care Suggestions
Keywords: preventive, screening, clinician, checkup, family history, follow-up, medication

Preventive cardiovascular care works best when the patient is monitored before symptoms escalate.

## Preventive steps
- Schedule routine clinical review for blood pressure, lipids, diabetes status, and medication adherence.
- Review family history, smoking status, and symptom changes during follow-up rather than waiting for emergencies.
- Keep vaccinations, sleep quality, and other general health maintenance on track because they affect resilience and recovery.

## Escalation points
- Worsening exertional symptoms, rising blood pressure, or new chest discomfort should trigger earlier review.
- Preventive plans should be revisited when multiple risk factors cluster together.
