# Emergency Warning Signs
Keywords: warning, emergency, chest pain, stroke, fainting, shortness of breath, urgent

Some cardiovascular symptoms should never be managed as a routine lifestyle issue.

## Seek urgent medical attention now for:
- Active chest pain or pressure that is severe, persistent, or spreading to the arm, jaw, or back.
- Sudden shortness of breath, fainting, or severe dizziness.
- Stroke-like symptoms such as facial droop, slurred speech, one-sided weakness, or sudden confusion.

## Do not delay
- If symptoms are new, intense, or rapidly worsening, urgent medical care is more important than self-management advice.
- Lifestyle changes help long-term risk but do not replace emergency evaluation during acute symptoms.
