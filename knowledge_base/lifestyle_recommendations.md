# Lifestyle Recommendations
Keywords: lifestyle, smoking, sleep, stress, prevention, cardiovascular, routine

Cardiovascular risk improves most when daily habits change consistently rather than temporarily.

## Core guidance
- Build a weekly routine that includes sleep consistency, movement, meal planning, and stress management.
- If the patient smokes, prioritize smoking cessation because it meaningfully reduces long-term cardiovascular risk.
- Limit heavy alcohol use and avoid relying on short bursts of motivation; use structured habits and reminders instead.

## Practical monitoring
- Pair lifestyle goals with measurable check-ins such as weight trends, blood pressure logs, and weekly activity totals.
- Reassess progress every few weeks so the plan can be adjusted before poor habits become the default again.
