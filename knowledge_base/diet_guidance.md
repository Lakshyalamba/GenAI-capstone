# Heart-Healthy Diet Guidance
Keywords: diet, nutrition, sodium, mediterranean, weight, bmi, diabetes, cholesterol

Diet quality strongly affects cardiovascular risk through blood pressure, cholesterol, weight, and glucose control.

## Meal pattern recommendations
- Build most meals around vegetables, fruit, legumes, whole grains, and lean protein sources.
- Prefer minimally processed foods and reduce routine intake of fried foods, refined snacks, and sugary drinks.
- Keep sodium in check, especially when blood pressure is elevated or there is fluid retention.

## Weight and glucose support
- A calorie pattern that supports gradual weight loss can improve both blood pressure and metabolic markers.
- Spread carbohydrates more evenly through the day if diabetes or insulin resistance is present.
