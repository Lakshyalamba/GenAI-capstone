# Cholesterol Management Guidance
Keywords: cholesterol, lipids, saturated fat, fiber, statin, diet, triglycerides

Total cholesterol should be managed in the context of overall cardiovascular risk, not as an isolated number.

## Dietary focus
- Replace fried foods and saturated fats with unsaturated fat sources such as nuts, seeds, olive oil, and fatty fish.
- Increase soluble fiber from oats, beans, lentils, fruit, and vegetables to support lipid control.
- Balance portion size and total calorie intake if excess weight is contributing to the cholesterol profile.

## Clinical follow-up
- Ask for repeat lipid testing on a schedule that matches the patient's overall risk profile.
- Discuss medication options if lifestyle changes alone are not enough for the patient's risk level.
